About Content Management

The Content Management system is designed to help organize and manage digital content effectively.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a simple, intuitive interface for content creators to manage their work efficiently.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React.js with Next.js framework
  • Backend: Express.js API
  • Database: File-based storage (JSON)

Integration

This system integrates with the Constellation Viewer to provide a comprehensive content ecosystem.
